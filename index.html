Waverindle

Lore

A creature made entirely of dense storm-fog, Waverindle flickers between dimensions whenever thunder rolls. It is visible only for moments — often mistaken for a trick of the light — but its sighs can be heard across empty fields.

Field Notes

  • Habitat:Thunderstorms
  • Primary Ability:Flickers between dimensions
  • Known Weakness:Silence (still, windless nights)
